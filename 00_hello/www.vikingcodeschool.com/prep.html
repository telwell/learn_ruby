Why we have prep work

We believe that the best engineers understand not just how to code but how to create effective and useful web applications as well. That means they should have a good understanding of the whole product lifecycle from design to deploy.

Our prep mini-courses will greatly improve your understanding of these fundamental concepts. They will not just prepare you for the interview in our application process but will help get the most from our program so you can emerge as a more successful and effective engineer.

How it works

These prep mini-courses draw from existing resources, new demos and special projects to help you learn the material. Each mini-course is designed to take a week or two to complete and will challenge you to actually use the concepts you've just learned.

After finishing all four mini-courses, you should be well prepared for our application process and success in our intensive program.

The Prep Mini-Courses

Web Development Basics

The basics of what a web developer does, how the web works, how to use your command line and Git.

Web Design Basics

The foundations of user experience (UX), visual design, and mocking up your sites using Balsamiq.

Software Engineering Basics

How software engineers solve complex problems and collaborate using agile development methods.

Web Markup and Coding

Building pages with HTML/CSS and solving technical challenges with the Ruby programming language.

These courses aren't required before you apply but they will help you to make it through our interview and to ultimately be successful in our program.
